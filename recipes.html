chocolate
chocolate milkshake

Ingredients

1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
whipped cream to your liking
2 tbs cream or milk
1-1/2 tbs chocolate syrup
Haagen Dazs chocolate ice cream

PREP TIME
2 MINUTES

TOTAL TIME
10 MINUTES

SERVES
2 PEOPLE

INSTRUCTIONS
step 1
combine chocolate and cream in a small microwave-safe bowl. heat on medium power 1 to 2 minutes until warm. stir until smooth, reheating to melt chocolate if needed. stirrum into warm chocolate. scoop ice cream into bowls and surround with sliced banana. top servings with about 2 tablespoons warm chocolate rum sauce and 1 tablespoon coconut.
